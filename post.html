Surviving S2 on Paracetamol and Anime

Written by Ashitha • May 12, 2026

If there's one thing S2 Civil Engineering has taught me, it's that coffee isn't always enough. Sometimes, you just need a good dose of paracetamol and a relaxing anime episode to get through the week.

"Engineering is 10% learning the formulas and 90% learning how to operate under extreme sleep deprivation."

Lately, my routine has been pretty simple: wake up, go to classes at Barton Hill, hang out with some campus dogs, and try to make sense of the syllabus. The Kick Off 6.0 event was a blast, and winning it gave me a massive confidence boost!

But when I'm home and the headache kicks in, I just tune out the world with my favorite music playlists. Whether it's the soothing OSTs from my top anime or just playing with dogs, you have to find those little pockets of peace.
